History

Royal Care Hospital is a renowned multi-specialty hospital located in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India. Here are some key features of Royal Care Hospital:

  1. Comprehensive Medical Care:
    • Royal Care offers compassionate and advanced medical care across various specialties.
    • Their mission is to provide sustained readership in scientific investigation and patient care.
    • The hospital caters to patients of all strata of society, including free treatment for the economically weaker sections.
  2. Specialties and Departments:
    • Vascular Surgery: Managing vascular conditions.
    • Anesthesiology: Preoperative care and anesthesia.
    • Cardiothoracic & Vascular Surgery: Specialized heart and vascular procedures.
    • Dental and Maxillofacial Surgery: Treating jaw, face, and mouth defects.
    • Dermatology and Cosmetology: Focusing on skin health.
    • Emergency Medicine: Treating a full spectrum of patients.
    • Endocrinology: Dealing with endocrine disorders.
    • ENT, Head & Neck Surgery: Addressing ear, nose, and throat issues.
    • Fetal Medicine: Monitoring unborn babies health.
    • Haematology: Treating blood disorders.
    • Critical Care Medicine: Providing high-quality care.
    • Interventional Cardiology: Diagnosing and treating heart conditions.
    • Interventional Radiology: Minimally invasive procedures.
    • Internal Medicine: Manned by experienced physicians.
    • Laboratory Medicine: Industry-approved medical lab equipment.
    • Master Health Check-Up: Comprehensive health assessments.
    • Medical Gastroenterology: Focusing on gastrointestinal diseases.
  3. International Patients:
    • Royal Care is recognized as the best multi-specialty hospital in Coimbatore.
    • They ensure world-class healthcare, teaching, training, and research.
    • The hospital provides cutting-edge technological equipment and renowned medical specialists
