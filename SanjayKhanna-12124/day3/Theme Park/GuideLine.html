Theme park safety guidelines

  1. Observe Ride Safety Rules:
    • Pay attention to all posted ride safety rules.
    • Follow verbal instructions given by ride operators or provided through recorded announcements.
    • Keep your hands, arms, legs, and feet inside the ride at all times.
  2. Secure Loose Articles:
    • Before boarding a ride, secure all loose articles such as wallets, change, sunglasses, cell phones, and hats.
    • These items can become hazardous during the ride if not properly secured.
  3. Avoid Boarding Impaired:
    • Do not board a ride if you are impaired due to alcohol, drugs, or any other reason.
