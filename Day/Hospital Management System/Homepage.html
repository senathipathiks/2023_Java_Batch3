About us

Founded in 1997 to provide quality critical care to communities in South Tamil Nadu, Apollo Specialty Hospitals, Madurai became a landmark in the city. With specialized medical experts and cutting-edge technology, the 300 bedded hospital offers affordable and world-class healthcare.

To ensure high standards of healthcare, the hospital has integrated excellent infrastructure, latest technology and dedicated manpower, to provide patient services of the highest standards. The hospital places special emphasis on quality time between patients, doctors and nurses.

The hospital has touched millions of lives and sees an average of 15,000 patients a year. With an overall success rate of 98%, the 24 hour panel of leading specialists is equipped to tackle any emergency and honour the hospital’s commitment to providing all services under one roof.
